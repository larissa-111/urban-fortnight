Sisig Recipe

About the Dish

Sisig is a famous Filipino dish made from pork, onions, and chili peppers, known for its sizzling and savory flavor. It is often served on a hot plate and enjoyed with rice or as a pulutan (beer match).

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 green chilies sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp calamansi or lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions for cooking

  1. Boil the pork belly until tender then grill or pan fry until crispy then chop into small pieces
  2. In a pan saut√© onion and garlic until fragrant
  3. Add the chopped pork soy sauce and calamansi juice then mix well
  4. Add the green chilies season with salt and pepper
  5. Serve on a sizzling plate crack the egg on top and mix before eating
